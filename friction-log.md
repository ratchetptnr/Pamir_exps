# PamirAI Device Friction Log

This document tracks friction points, usability issues, and improvement opportunities discovered during PamirAI device testing.

## How to Use This Log

When documenting friction, include:
- **Date**: When the issue was encountered
- **Category**: Setup, Hardware, Software, UX, Documentation, etc.
- **Severity**: Low, Medium, High, Critical
- **Description**: What happened and what was expected
- **Context**: What you were trying to accomplish
- **Impact**: How this affects the user experience
- **Suggestions**: Potential solutions or improvements
